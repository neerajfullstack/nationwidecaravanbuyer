About Nationwide Caravan Buyer

At Nationwide Caravan Buyer we make the process of selling your caravan quick, simple and secure. Our expert valuations team will take into account any factory fitted options and accessories included to get you the best possible price.

We buy for the UK market and for export to Australia and New Zealand. From your initial contact to the completion of the sale our aim is to make the process hassle free, speedy and secure. Our payments are made via BACS transfer so that you have the money in the bank instantly.
